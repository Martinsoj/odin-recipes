Easy Chicken Stir fry for my cute baby

Description

This quick and easy chicken stir fry is perfect for busy weeknights when you need a nutritious meal on the table fast. Tender pieces of chicken breast are tossed with colorful vegetables in a savory sauce that brings restaurant-quality flavour to your home kitchen

The beauty of this recipes is its versatility-feel free to substitute with whatever vegetables you have on hand. Serve over rice or noodles for a complete meal that's ready in just 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups white rice
  • ⅔ cup low-sodium soy sauce, or to taste
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Meanwhile, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch in a medium glass or ceramic bowl; stir until smooth.
  3. Serve over rice.
